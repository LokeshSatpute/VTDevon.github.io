Design Rationale

The design of EventSphere aims to deliver a comprehensive, modular, and scalable event management platform that meets the diverse needs of organizers, attendees, and vendors. This system integrates various services such as ticketing, payment processing, order management, real-time vendor coordination, and analytics into a seamless experience. EventSphere leverages event-driven architecture to maintain responsiveness during high-demand scenarios, ensuring smooth operations across multiple services. The platform is structured to support high availability, secure transactions, and real-time updates, essential for large-scale events with fluctuating traffic and operational demands. By adopting a layered architecture and domain-driven design (DDD), the system ensures that each function, from ticket generation to post-event reporting, operates independently but cohesively. These design decisions promote maintainability and flexibility, allowing for easy enhancements and future scalability.
